About Genesis

AN EVENT THAT CONNECTS, INSPIRES AND BRINGS YOUTH TOGETHER

Genesis'19 brings together some of the best talent of the country. Every year, around 8000-10000 student's crowd in the campus of MAIMS, Rohini to participate in the GENESIS - Annual festival which is the largest annual cultural event in Rohini. More than 800 artists and exhibitors participate and perform enthusiastically making a GENESIS a mega successful event. One of the best colleges like IIT, NSIT, AMITY and other colleges of IPU and DU etc are participants of the event.

About Maims

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Management Studies (MAIMS) is an ambitious initiative of Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society. The society is involved in various activities relating to academia, health care and has established institutions including

  • Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology, Rohini, Delhi
  • A Medical college in Agroha (Haryana)
  • Several polytechnics,
  • Maharaja Agrasen University at Baddi, Himachal Pradesh

MAIMS, affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. it was established in year 2003 to impart high quality education in management and journalism.

  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • B.com (Hons.)
  • Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication [BA(JMC)]
  • BA (Hons.) Economics
  • BA LLB (Integrated) programmes

IPL AUCTION

Rules

ROUND 1
THIS WILL BE A QUIZ ROUND. THERE WILL BE A TOTAL OF 20 QUESTIONS FROM IPL WHICH WILL INCLUDE BOTH MCQ’S AND SUBJECTIVE TYPE QUESTIONS. EACH TEAM WILL BE GIVEN POINTS ON THE BASIS OF THE QUESTIONS CORRECTLY ANSWERED BY THEM AND THERE WILL BE NO NEGATIVE MARKING. A TOTAL OF 16 TEAMS WILL GO THROUGH THIS ROUND.

ROUND 2
THIS WILL BE ANOTHER QUIZ ROUND INCLUDING QUESTIONS FROM WORLD CRICKET OVERALL. THE FORMAT OF THIS ROUND WILL BE THE SAME AS THE FIRST ROUND. 8 TEAMS WILL GO THROUGH THIS ROUND.

ROUND 3
THIS WILL BE THE ROUND OF THE IPL AUCTION. EACH OF THE SELECTED TEAM WILL BE GIVEN A CHOICE TO SELECT ONE OF THE 8 IPL TEAMS ACCORDING TO THEIR STANDING IN ROUND 2. THE FORMAT OF THE AUCTION WILL BE EXACTLY THE SAME AS IT IS DONE IN THE REAL AUCTION.
IN THE END THE TEAM WITH MOST NUMBER OF POINTS ACCORDING TO THE GIVEN CRITERION WILL SECURE THE FIRST POSITION. THERE WILL BE THREE WINNER TEAMS.

BASIC RULES- AUCTION

A PURSE OF 80CR WILL BE GIVEN TO EACH TEAM
EACH TEAM CAN RETAIN 5 PLAYERS WITH A COMBINATION OF MAXIMUM 3 RETENTIONS AND 2 RIGHT TO MATCH CARDS. MAXIMUM 3 INDIAN CAPPED PLAYERS AND 2 FOREIGN CAPPED PLAYERS CAN BE RETAINED

BID INCREMENTS-
UPTO 1CR- 5 LAKHS
UPTO 2CR-10 LAKHS
UPTO 10CR-20 LAKHS
ABOVE 10 CR-50 LAKHS

TEAM COMPOSITION-

AT LEAST 3 BATSMEN
